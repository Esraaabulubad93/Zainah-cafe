Our Story


For a truly fun dining experience, you will want to take your family and friends to Lemon

The Eatery – which is famous for its delicious signature dishes, friendly staff, extensive beverage list and shisha
keeps visitors entertained with TV screens featuring live sports tournaments, card and board games.

Founded by EAT Restaurant Group, Lemon opened its doors in 2008. Over the next few years,
through the addition of other branches in Amman, Irbid, Ramallah, and Bahrain, Lemon would quickly become
a local and regional favorite to legions of loyal customers.

Serving a hearty breakfast spread, as well as a flavorful lunch and dinner menu, the trendy
eatery is the perfect spot to indulge in mouthwatering dessert and made-to-order juices, cocktails,
and smoothies.

The restaurant’s contemporary, comfortable ambiance features fresh colors, plush seats,
and a lush outdoor patio. The bistro further has a dedicated non-smoking section.
